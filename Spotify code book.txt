- `artist_name`: The name of the artist, to the song streamed. 
- `track_name`: The title of the track streamed. 
- `date`: The date at which the song was streamed. 
- `seconds`: How many seconds I streamed the song for. 
- `minutes`: How many minutes I streamed the song for.  
- `skipped`: Whether or not I skipped or streamed the song depending on it's playtime.
